Letter to a Filipino penfriend

Dear Ms Nicole,

How are you? I hope you are doing well. I'm writing to share some exciting news with you. Recently, I've moved to a new school in Ho Chi Minh city.

At first, I felt a little nervous because everything was unfamiliar - new teachers, new classmates, and a different learning environtment. However, everyone here is very friendly and supportive, so I'm starting to adapt easily and feel more comfortable.

The school is quite big, with modern classrooms, a library, and even a footbal field. I especially enjoy English and Physical Education classes. I have also made a few new friends, and we often study and play together after school.

Although I miss my old school and friends, I'm happy with this new beginning. I believe it will give me many new experiences and opportunities.

How about you? How are your teachings going? I'm really looking forward to hearing from you soon.

Best wishes,
Phu Vinh
